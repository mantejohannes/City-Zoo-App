LOST FOREST

Step into the mysterious and lush world of the Lost Forest — a quiet, shaded area filled with dense greenery, hidden creatures, and winding paths. This immersive experience is designed to mimic a tropical rainforest, offering a peaceful escape and a chance to observe some of nature’s most secretive animals.

As you wander through the forest, keep an eye out for lemurs, chameleons, exotic birds, and small forest mammals. The thick canopy above and damp forest floor below provide a cool, calming atmosphere, perfect for both quiet reflection and educational exploration.

Interpretive signs guide you along the trail, sharing stories about rainforest biodiversity, conservation challenges, and the importance of preserving these vital ecosystems.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Lost Forest is located behind the Amphitheatre, tucked between pens E15 and F05.
