RESTAURANT

Sit down, relax, and enjoy a full dining experience at our family-friendly restaurant. With a wide-ranging menu that includes hearty meals, light bites, and traditional favourites, there's something for everyone to enjoy.

Choose from freshly prepared dishes such as grilled meats, vegetarian plates, pastas, salads, and daily chef specials. The restaurant also offers a kids’ menu, desserts, and a selection of hot and cold beverages.

With comfortable indoor seating and a scenic outdoor terrace overlooking the gardens, it’s the perfect place to enjoy a leisurely lunch or an early dinner during your visit.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The restaurant is situated near the centre of the park, close to pen D05 and the Reptile House.
