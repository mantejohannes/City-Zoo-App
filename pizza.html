JO'S PIZZA

Craving something cheesy and satisfying? Stop by Jo's Pizza for a delicious slice of freshly made pizza! From classic Margherita and Pepperoni to veggie and chicken options, there’s something to please every palate.

All our pizzas are made with fresh ingredients and baked on-site. You can grab a quick slice or order a full pizza to share with family and friends. Jo’s also offers cold drinks, garlic rolls, and tasty sides to complete your meal.

It's the perfect stop for a filling lunch or a late-afternoon snack as you explore the park.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

Jo’s Pizza is located next to the playground area, just a short walk from pen C08.
